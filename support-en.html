Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aminmart Password Manager?

Aminmart Password Manager is an offline password management application designed to store and secure your passwords without needing an internet connection. Your data remains on your own device and is never uploaded to any server.

Is it safe to use this app?

Yes, it's very safe. The app uses AES-256 encryption to protect sensitive data like passwords and notes. Since it's offline, there's no risk of your data being stolen through the internet. Data is stored locally on your device only.

How do I add a new password?

Open the app and tap the "+" or "Add" button on the main screen. Fill in the account details such as title, username, password, and website. You can also assign categories and add additional notes if needed.

Can I use biometric features?

Yes, the app supports fingerprint and face recognition authentication (if your device supports it). You can enable this feature in the settings menu to unlock the app more quickly and securely.

What if I forget my master password?

Because the app is offline and has no central server, there's no way to recover a forgotten master password. If you forget your master password, the only solution is to clear the app data and start over. Be sure to back up important data regularly.

Is the app available for iOS?

Currently, Aminmart Password Manager is only available for Android devices. An iOS version is under development and will be released in the future.

Troubleshooting

App won't open

  • Restart your device and try opening the app again
  • Check if the app has the required permissions
  • Clear the app cache through device settings
  • If the problem persists, reinstall the app

Biometric feature not working

  • Make sure fingerprint/face features are enabled in device settings
  • Check if the biometric sensor is clean and undamaged
  • Disable and re-enable biometric features in app settings
  • Ensure the app has permission to access biometric features
